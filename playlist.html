About the App

The PC Configuration Tool App is an online platform that enables users to build their own custom PC configurations by selecting individual computer parts such as CPUs, motherboards, graphics cards, memory, storage, and more. It gets this information by importing a database that has all the information on each component. The app also displays the prices of each component and the total price of the configuration after adding it to the cart.

The app is built using Java and Object-Oriented Design principles to ensure efficient and scalable development.

How it Works

The app works by displaying a catalog of computer parts from various manufacturers and allowing users to select their desired components. As users add components to their configuration, the app displays the price of each component and calculates the total price of the configuration

The app also includes a feature to save and load configurations, allowing users to come back and modify their configuration at a later time.

Why Use the App

The PC Configuration Tool App can be used by anyone who wants to build a custom PC but may not have the technical knowledge to do so. The app simplifies the process by presenting the user with a clear list of compatible parts and displaying the prices of each component.

The app can also be used by computer enthusiasts and professionals who want to compare prices and build more advanced configurations. The app's save and load feature allows users to experiment with different configurations and compare prices before making a final purchase decision. Additionally, the app's use of Object-Oriented Design ensures that the app is easy to maintain and scale, making it a reliable tool for building custom PCs.
