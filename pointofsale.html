About the App

The Grocery Point of Sale System is a software application that enables grocery stores to manage their sales and inventory. It includes a database of grocery items and their prices, which can be updated as needed. The app allows users to add items to the cart and calculates the total price of the order.

The app is built using Java and Object-Oriented Programming principles, ensuring reliable and scalable development.

How it Works

The app works by displaying a catalog of grocery items from the database and allowing users to select the desired items. As users add items to the cart, the app calculates the total price of the order and displays it to the user.

The app also includes inventory management features that allow store managers to track inventory levels and restock items as needed.

Why Use the App

TThe Grocery Point of Sale System can be used by grocery stores of any size to manage their sales and inventory. The app simplifies the checkout process by automatically calculating the total price of the order, reducing errors and increasing efficiency.

The app can also be used to track inventory levels and identify popular items, making it easier for store managers to make informed decisions about restocking and promotions. The app's use of Object-Oriented Programming ensures that the app is easy to maintain and scale, making it a reliable tool for managing grocery sales and inventory.
